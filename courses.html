Courses & Workshops

The Crossroad of Exploring Geospatial Science: Learning by Sharing Knowledge

Organizer: GroupMappers

Duration: 1 day | Year: 2022

This workshop offered an intensive one-day program focused on the fundamentals of geospatial science. Participants engaged in interactive sessions and collaborative discussions to explore modern techniques in geospatial analysis.

Mauza Georeferencing and Digitizing using ArcGIS

Organizer: GMGI Solutions Ltd.

Duration: 1 day | Year: 2022

This practical workshop focused on transforming traditional maps into digital formats through georeferencing and digitization techniques using ArcGIS. It enhanced skills in spatial data conversion and geoprocessing for accurate mapping.

How to Write a Research Article and Thesis Paper

Organizer: Research Tech Hub

Duration: 2 hours | Year: 2024

This short course provided essential guidelines for academic writing, covering the structure, style, and best practices for drafting research articles and thesis papers. It aimed to help participants enhance their scholarly communication skills.
