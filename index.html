A Bit About Me
Hello! My name is MD Talha. I am currently pursuing my master's degree at the Department of Geography and Environment, Jagannath University, where my final exams are ongoing. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong passion for research, which has led me to actively engage in multiple research projects. My first article was published in September 2024, and since then, I have successfully published four journal articles in Q1 and Q2 international journals, along with a book chapter in a reputed book series.

I am particularly interested in exploring environmental and urban issues through research, leveraging my expertise in GIS, disaster management, and urban climate studies. My dedication to academic excellence and research has not only strengthened my analytical and problem-solving skills but also deepened my understanding of real-world challenges.

Currently, I am seeking an academic role that aligns with my research interests and allows me to contribute meaningfully to knowledge generation. I am confident that my research expertise, commitment, and innovative approach can add significant value to any institution I become a part of. My goal is to establish a strong academic and research presence, working collaboratively to drive impactful studies and solutions.
